# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][cc].

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, focus on the work. Disagreement
about technique, wording, or scope is welcome; personal attacks and harassment
are not.

## Reporting

Concerns about a contributor's conduct can be reported privately to the
maintainers at **`mnikitin@itsalt.ru`**. Reports will be reviewed promptly and
handled in confidence.

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
